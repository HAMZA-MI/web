Cultural Heritage of Sbeitla

About the Historical Site:

Sbeitla is one of Tunisia’s most prominent historical cities, known for its impressive Roman archaeological site. The most iconic features are the three Roman temples dedicated to the gods Jupiter, Juno, and Minerva. Dating back to the 1st century AD, these monuments reflect the grandeur of Roman civilization in North Africa.
